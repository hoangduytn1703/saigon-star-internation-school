Vision & Guiding Statements

Nurturing Global Citizens

Saigon Star International School is a premier educational institution located in the heart of Ho Chi Minh City. With a strong commitment to holistic development, the school provides a nurturing environment where learners can flourish academically, socially, and emotionally. Combining the best of British and international curricula, SGSIS offers a well-rounded education that equips students with the skills and knowledge to succeed in a globalized world.

Vision

All children are helped to become globally-competent, well-rounded, lifelong learners.

Guiding Statements

We provide motivating and engaging learning experiences, within a safe and nurturing environment, that enable every child to experience daily success in learning.

A Journey of Growth and Achievement

From its humble beginnings to becoming a leading international school in Ho Chi Minh City,
Saigon Star has experienced remarkable growth and success. Key milestones include:

Founded with a vision to offer world-class education in Vietnam.
2005
2010
Moved to a state-of-the-art campus, providing a stimulating learning environment.
Adopted the International Primary Curriculum (IPC) to enhance holistic development.
2013
2016
Pioneered the implementation of the International Early Years Curriculum (IEYC).
Achieved the prestigious status of being the first and only IPC-accredited school in Vietnam.
2018
A Community of Care

SGSIS is more than just a school. It's a nurturing community where students, teachers, and parents come together to create a shared passion for learning. Our dedicated faculty, coupled with our supportive parent community, have been instrumental in shaping the school's success.
