Accrediation

Saigon Star International School (SGSIS) is a proud member of the International Curriculum Association (ICA).This affiliation demonstrates SGSIS's commitment to providing a high-quality international education that meets global standards. As a member of the ICA, SGSIS benefits from

Global Recognition

ICA membership gives SGSIS international credibility and recognition.

Professional Development

The ICA offers a range of professional development opportunities for SGSIS teachers and staff.

Networking

SGSIS can connect with other international schools and share best practices.

By being a part of the ICA, SGSIS ensures that its students receive a well-rounded education
that prepares them for success in a globalized world.

For further information about ICA, visit www.internationalcurriculum.com
