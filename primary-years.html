SGSIS: A Pioneer in
International Education

Saigon Star International School (SGSIS) is proud to be the first and only school in Vietnam to achieve accreditation with the International Primary Curriculum (IPC).

This prestigious accreditation offers students a unique opportunity to engage in a world-class curriculum that fosters critical thinking, creativity, and a global perspective. By studying in the IPC program, students gain valuable skills and knowledge that prepare them for success in a rapidly changing world.

Our primary program is a unique blend of the British National Curriculum and the International Primary Curriculum (IPC). This innovative approach ensures that our students receive a well-rounded education, encompassing both academic excellence and personal development. Our curriculum offers:

Strong foundation in core subjects:

A rigorous program in English, mathematics, science, and other essential areas.

Tailored sporting and creative opportunities:

A variety of activities to foster physical fitness, artistic expression, and social skills.

Personalised support:

A nurturing environment that helps each student reach their full potential.

International Primary Curriculum (IPC):
A Global Approach to Primary Education

The International Primary Curriculum (IPC) is a comprehensive and engaging curriculum designed to meet the needs of primary-aged children worldwide. Developed by a global team of educators, the IPC aims to provide a high-quality, relevant, and culturally sensitive education that prepares students for the challenges and opportunities of the 21st century.

Global Perspective

The IPC fosters a global understanding by incorporating international themes and perspectives into the curriculum.

Learner-Centered Approach

The curriculum focuses on the individual needs & interests of students, providing a personalized and engaging learning experience.

Culturally Responsive

The IPC values diversity and respects different cultures, ensuring that all students feel included and valued.

Inquiry-Based Learning

Students are encouraged to investigate and explore the world around them through inquiry-based learning, promoting critical thinking and problem-solving skills.

Integrated Learning

The IPC integrates subjects such as English, mathematics, science, history, geography, and the arts to create meaningful connections between different areas of knowledge.

Learning Journey & Events

Entry Point

Entry Points are exciting events at the beginning of each new topic, designed to excite and enthuse children, and generate more engagement (and more learning throughout the topic). They can last anywhere between 1 hour and an entire day.

Exit Point

Exit points occur at the end of each topic and are a fantastic opportunity for children to showcase their learning to parents, and other learners and teachers across the school.

Learning Showcases

Learning Showcases take place most Friday mornings, from 8.30-9.00am. Teachers sign up as and when they want to, often at the end of a topic (as it could be considered an Exit Point, demonstrating their knowledge and understanding of a Big Idea). Parents from the leading class are invited to attend.

A Nature-Nurtured Learning Experience

At SGSIS, students benefit immensely from daily playtime in the school's expansive 7,500m² green space. This immersive experience in nature fosters a deep connection to the environment, promotes physical and emotional well-being, and sparks curiosity and creativity. As students explore, discover, and interact with the natural world, they develop a strong foundation for lifelong learning and appreciation for the planet.

A Stimulating Learning Environment

The standard facilities room for primary students at SGSIS is designed to foster a stimulating and engaging learning environment. Equipped with age-appropriate furniture, interactive learning materials, and ample natural light, the room provides a comfortable and inspiring space for young children to explore, discover, and grow. The facilities support a variety of activities, from sport fields, science labs, music and dance room,  art and crafts to language development and sensory exploration, ensuring a well-rounded and enriching educational experience.

International Dedicated Teachers

At SGSIS, our dedicated and experienced teachers are committed to nurturing early years students with care, patience, and enthusiasm. They create a safe and supportive learning environment where children can feel confident to explore, ask questions, and express themselves freely. Our teachers use engaging and age-appropriate teaching methods to foster a love of learning and prepare students for future success.
